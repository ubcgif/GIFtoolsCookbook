Load Predicted Data
===================

Once the forward model has run, the user may load the predicted data. Click on the forward modeling item, select the menu showing its class (e.g., ``E3Dfoward`` or ``GRAVforward``):

- Load all results: **[Forward modeling class]** |rarr| **Load predicted data**
